Volunteer with Corbin Senior Activity Center

At Corbin Senior Activity Center, volunteers are the heart of everything we do. By giving your time and talents, you help us create a warm, welcoming community where seniors can stay active, engaged, and connected. Volunteering is not only a way to give back—it’s also a chance to make meaningful friendships, learn new skills, and make a real difference in the lives of others. We offer a variety of volunteer opportunities to suit your interests and schedule, including driving our community bus, assisting with office work, helping in the kitchen, or supporting special events through setup, decorations, and cleanup. Whether you have a few hours a week or can help at occasional events, your contribution helps Corbin continue to thrive and serve the community. Join us in making every day brighter at Corbin—because when you volunteer, you’re not just helping others; you’re enriching your own life too.

What We Need

  • Bus Drivers
  • Office Work
  • Events
  • Set Up Events
  • Tear Down Events
  • Kitchen
