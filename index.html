Welcome to Corbin Senior Activity Center

At Corbin Senior Activity Center, every day brings new opportunities to explore, grow, and connect. From group fitness and dance classes to educational workshops, travel adventures, game nights, quilting circles, and social events, we offer something for everyone. Whether you’re here to stay active, discover a new passion, or simply enjoy good company — you’ll find your place at Corbin.

Events

Experience the vibrancy of life at Corbin Senior Activity Center! Join us for a calendar filled with lively events tailored just for you. From enriching workshops to spirited social gatherings, there's always something happening at Corbin. Embrace the joy of community, forge new connections, and make every moment count. Browse our upcoming events and mark your calendar for a season of laughter, learning, and shared memories. Your next adventure awaits at Corbin Senior Activity Center!

Tours

Embark on a journey of discovery with Corbin Tours. Our curated excursions blend adventure with comfort, offering a unique exploration for our vibrant senior community. Whether it's a scenic day trip, a cultural outing, or an extended travel experience, Corbin Tours provides a gateway to new horizons. Join fellow explorers as we create lasting memories together. From local gems to far-off destinations, let Corbin Tours be your passport to a world of possibilities. Discover, connect, and travel with us – because the best adventures are shared.

Everyday Corbin

Every day is a new opportunity for connection and joy at Corbin Senior Activity Center. Engage in a variety of daily activities crafted to enrich your mind, body, and spirit. From morning fitness classes that invigorate to afternoon pinochle sessions, there's something for everyone. Join friends for lively card games, savor delicious communal meals, and discover the joy of shared experiences. At Corbin, each day is a celebration of community, laughter, and the simple pleasures of life. Embrace the routine of fulfillment. Your daily adventure starts here.

Community Partners

At Corbin Senior Activity Center, we believe in the strength of community collaboration. Our journey is enriched by the valuable partnerships we've formed with local businesses, organizations, and individuals. Together, we create a network of support, offering a range of services and resources to enhance the lives of our seniors. These meaningful collaborations reflect our shared commitment to fostering a vibrant and caring community. Join us in celebrating the power of unity as we continue to grow and thrive in partnership with the wonderful organizations that make Corbin Senior Activity Center a hub of support and connection.
