Experience the Extraordinary: Welcome to Everyday Corbin

Welcome to Everyday Corbin, your ultimate destination for endless excitement and enrichment! At Corbin Senior Activity Center, we’re proud to offer a vibrant array of activities to fill your days with joy, laughter, and unforgettable experiences. Embark on thrilling Day Tours that whisk you away to fascinating destinations, or dive into the adventure of Long Tours that promise unforgettable memories and new friendships. Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or eager to explore closer to home, our tours offer something for everyone. For those who love the thrill of competition, our games will keep you on the edge of your seat with friendly competition and lively camaraderie. Challenge your mind and test your skills with a variety of games tailored to suit all interests and skill levels. Stay active and energized with our dynamic exercise classes, designed to promote physical well-being and vitality. From invigorating workouts to gentle stretches, there’s a class to suit every fitness level and preference. Unleash your creativity and connect with fellow enthusiasts in our quilting groups, where passion and craftsmanship converge to create beautiful works of art. Whether you’re a seasoned quilter or just starting out, you’ll find inspiration and support in our welcoming community. And let’s not forget the magic of tap dancing – a timeless art form that celebrates rhythm, movement, and expression. Join us on the dance floor and tap into your inner performer as you learn new steps and master the art of dance. But the excitement doesn’t stop there! With a never-ending list of activities and events, there’s always something happening at Corbin. From educational workshops to social gatherings, there’s never a dull moment in our bustling community. So why wait? Come join us at Everyday Corbin and discover the thrill of living life to the fullest. Your next adventure awaits!

Corbin Tours

Get ready to embark on the journey of a lifetime with Corbin Tours – where every trip is an adventure waiting to unfold! Whether you’re craving a quick getaway or yearning for a grand expedition, we’ve got you covered with our exhilarating Day Tours and epic Long Tours.

Join us each month for our Day Tours, where the fun never stops and the destinations are endless! Explore hidden gems and local treasures with our Breakfast Bunch and International Lunch tours led by the fabulous Virginia. Indulge your taste buds as we venture to charming breakfast nooks and exotic eateries, immersing ourselves in culinary delights from around the globe. And don’t miss out on the excitement of Happy Hour with the one and only Carol – each outing promises a new venue and a toast to good times!

But wait, there’s more! Prepare for the ultimate thrill with NW Treasures, a mystery tour led by the enigmatic Jan. With destinations shrouded in secrecy until the day of departure, every NW Treasures excursion is a delightful surprise just waiting to be uncovered.

And if you’re craving a longer escapade, look no further than Corbin’s Long Tours! Strap in for a whirlwind of excitement as we journey to the coast, take on Branson’s bright lights, or immerse ourselves in the rich culture of Japan. With Corbin and our esteemed travel partners – including Collette, Globus, and Friendly Excursions – the world is truly yours to explore.

So pack your bags, grab your sense of adventure, and join us for a year filled with unforgettable experiences. With Corbin Tours, the journey is just beginning – and the memories last a lifetime!

Let's Play

Get ready to dive into a world of endless fun and excitement at Corbin! With a wide array of activities catering to every interest, there’s never a dull moment in our bustling community.

Challenge your friends to a showdown on the virtual lanes with Wii Bowling, or gather ’round the table for intense rounds of pinochle, Mexican train, bridge, cribbage, or Scrabble. With so many options, the competition is always fierce and the laughter never-ending!

But wait – the fun doesn’t stop there! Join us for Mexican Train Tuesday and Pinochle Wednesday, where the parking lot is always packed and the competition heats up as we battle it out for victory. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a newcomer to the game, there’s a place for you at the table.

Feeling crafty? Dive into the world of quilting with our Sandbox Sewers, where creativity knows no bounds and every stitch tells a story. And for those with a passion for crochet, we’ve got you covered too – join us and unleash your inner artist as you create beautiful works of art with yarn and hook.

With our jam-packed Activities Calendar, there’s always something exciting happening at Corbin. From lively games to creative pursuits, there’s never a shortage of ways to connect, learn, and have fun.

And if you’re in the mood for some laid-back conversation, join us for Coffee and Conversation – a dedicated time and place to relax, unwind, and connect with fellow members over a cup of joe.

So come on over and join the fun at Corbin – where every day is a new adventure and the memories are yours to make!

Get Fit

Welcome to Corbin’s Health and Fitness Hub – where wellness meets vitality and every class is a step towards a healthier you! At Corbin, we believe that staying active and taking care of your health is key to living life to the fullest. That’s why we’re thrilled to offer a wide range of health classes designed to cater to every fitness level and interest.

Get ready to stretch, tone, and groove your way to fitness with our Stretch and Tone classes! Led by our energetic instructors, these classes are designed to keep our seniors moving and feeling their best.

Looking to find your inner zen? Look no further than our yoga offerings, including traditional yoga, chair yoga for those needing extra support, and Zumba – a high-energy dance workout that’s guaranteed to get your heart pumping. Led by our wonderful instructor Danielle, these classes are sure to leave you feeling fit, limber, and ready to take on the world.

But that’s not all – ACT 2 offers a variety of health and fitness classes right here at Corbin, including Stay Active and Independent Living (SAIL), chair yoga, and tai chi. Led by expert instructors, these classes are designed to improve balance, flexibility, and overall well-being.

And let’s not forget our newest addition – Strength and Endurance, taught by Phil, our body weight expert. This dynamic class is perfect for those looking to build strength, increase endurance, and take their fitness to the next level.

For those on a weight loss journey, we’ve got you covered with T.O.P.S (Taking Off the Pounds Sensibly) – a supportive and encouraging group focused on healthy, sustainable weight loss.

At Corbin, we believe that health is wealth – and we’re committed to helping you achieve your fitness goals every step of the way. So why wait? Join us today and discover the transformative power of health and fitness at Corbin.

Let's Dance

Step into the rhythm of life at Corbin, where dance opportunities abound and every step is a celebration! Whether you’re a seasoned pro or taking your first twirl on the dance floor, we’ve got something for everyone to enjoy.

Join us throughout the week and witness the magic of the Tap Grandmas as they dazzle and delight in the Henderson Room. From beginners to experts, our tap classes welcome dancers of all levels with open arms, inviting you to join in the fun and prepare for future performances.

Tuesday nights come alive with the expert guidance of renowned dance instructor Roger Carney. From salsa to swing, Roger teaches all forms of dance with passion and precision, ensuring every dancer finds their groove and shines on the dance floor.

On Fridays, kick up your heels and learn the art of line dancing with Sandy. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a newcomer to the dance floor, Sandy’s infectious energy and expert instruction will have you mastering the steps in no time, ready to tear up any line with style and flair.

And if you’re simply looking to let loose and have a ball, join us every Monday night for our senior-friendly dance extravaganza. With lively music, friendly faces, and plenty of room to mingle, it’s the perfect way to dance the night away and make memories that last a lifetime.

So if dance is your passion, look no further than Corbin. With a variety of classes and events designed to cater to every dancer’s dream, there’s no better place to lace up your dancing shoes and let the music move you. Come join us and discover the joy of dance at Corbin today!
