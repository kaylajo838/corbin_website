Unforgettable Moments, Endless Possibilities: Host Your Event at Corbin Senior Activity Center

Create timeless memories at Corbin Senior Activity Center with our versatile rental spaces tailored for every occasion. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we offer a range of options to suit your needs. Explore our spacious dining hall and fully-equipped kitchen, ideal for birthdays, weddings, and special events. Elevate your experience in the elegant Henderson Room, available as a complete package or as individual rentals. For more intimate affairs or business meetings, our three classrooms provide a comfortable and flexible environment. The Carolyn Bryan Suite, available in North and South configurations, offers a touch of sophistication and can be rented together or separately. Additionally, the Lower South Classroom provides a cozy setting for smaller gatherings. At Corbin, we understand that each event is unique. Our customizable packages and flexible pricing ensure that you have the freedom to create the event of your dreams within your budget. Contact us for more information, check availability, and book your date. Let Corbin Senior Activity Center be the canvas for your next celebration – where every detail reflects your vision and every moment becomes a cherished memory.

Henderson Room

Welcome to the heart of Corbin Senior Activity Center’s vibrant spirit: The Henderson Room, where every event becomes an unforgettable celebration!

Step into 1520 square feet of pure versatility, designed to host your wildest dreams, from jubilant parties to heartfelt memorials, electrifying concerts, and dazzling shows. As the crown jewel of Corbin, the Henderson Room reigns supreme, beckoning guests with its unmatched allure and endless possibilities.

Immerse yourself in elegance on our pristine hardwood flooring, under the soft glow of exquisite stained glass windows and ambient lighting that sets the perfect mood for any occasion. Take center stage on our spacious platform, where your performances shine brighter and your speeches resonate deeper.

With a capacity to accommodate up to 183 guests, revel in the company of friends, family, and colleagues, surrounded by the warmth and charm of our inviting atmosphere. Our 60-inch round tables, each a hub of laughter and connection, comfortably seat up to 8, while banquet tables stand ready to serve your every need.

Unlock the magic of your next event at the Henderson Room—where moments become memories, and celebrations reach new heights. Book your experience today and let the festivities begin!

Carolyn Bryan Suite North & South

Welcome to the epitome of versatility and charm: the Carolyn Bryan Suite North & South—where every detail is crafted to perfection, and every event becomes an extraordinary affair!

Step into a world of endless possibilities as you explore these meticulously designed spaces, ideal for meetings, classes, intimate gatherings, and beyond. Whether you choose to rent the North or the South individually, or combine them for an unforgettable experience, the Carolyn Bryan Suite promises to exceed your every expectation.

Immerse yourself in the convenience of modern amenities, with a TV available in both the North and South sections to enhance your presentations and entertainment. Need refreshments? The North boasts a mini fridge and microwave, while each side features a convenient working sink for added convenience.

As if tailored for bridal bliss, the South is uniquely equipped with a dedicated area, perfect for pre-wedding preparations and cherished moments with loved ones. Picture-perfect memories await in this haven of elegance and grace.

The Carolyn Bryan Suite North, spanning 832 square feet, invites up to 62 guests to indulge in its spacious comfort, while the South, at 730 square feet, accommodates up to 52 guests with effortless style. Each side comes complete with 8 ft tables, comfortable seating,, and of course, that essential touch of connectivity with a TV and sink.

But why settle for just one when you can have it all? Rent the North and South together to unlock the full potential of this dynamic duo, accommodating up to 200 guests in seamless harmony.

Elevate your next event to new heights at the Carolyn Bryan Suite North & South—where every moment is a masterpiece waiting to be celebrated. Reserve your slice of splendor today and let the magic unfold!

Dining Hall & Kitchen

Welcome to the heart of culinary delight and intimate gatherings: the Dining Hall and Kitchen at Corbin Senior Activity Center—where every meal becomes a cherished memory and every event a feast for the senses!

Step into our inviting Dining Hall, where warmth and intimacy blend seamlessly to create the perfect setting for your next celebration. From cozy birthdays to elegant retirement parties, exclusive dinners, and cherished family gatherings, this versatile space sets the stage for unforgettable moments.

Prepare to embark on a culinary journey like no other as you step into our state-of-the-art kitchen, equipped with an industrial convection oven, a six-burner stove, and an industrial dishwasher. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a culinary enthusiast, our kitchen is your canvas to create culinary masterpieces that will leave your guests craving for more.

With 639 square feet of dining space and a capacity for 49 guests, the Dining Hall offers an intimate setting where every conversation flows freely and every laughter resonates warmly. Whether you choose to rent it separately for a cozy affair or pair it with the grandeur of the Henderson Room for a larger event, the Dining Hall promises an experience that delights the senses and leaves a lasting impression.

Indulge in the art of hospitality at the Dining Hall and Kitchen—where culinary excellence meets heartfelt hospitality. Reserve your spot today and let the festivities begin in style!

Lower South

Welcome to the dynamic hub of learning and collaboration: the Lower South Class Room at Corbin Senior Activity Center—where every gathering becomes an opportunity to inspire, educate, and connect!

Step into a space designed to foster creativity and engagement, perfect for classes, seminars, and group meetings of all kinds. With 730 square feet of rental space and a capacity for up to 52 individuals, the Lower South Class Room offers ample room for exploration and discovery.

Equipped with everything you need for productive sessions, including 6 ft tables, chairs, a sink for convenience, a versatile whiteboard for brainstorming, and extra card tables for added flexibility. Whether you’re hosting a workshop, a training session, or a brainstorming meeting, this versatile space adapts effortlessly to your needs.

But the Lower South Class Room isn’t just about business—it’s also a place to celebrate and connect. From smaller parties to intimate get-togethers, this adaptable space sets the stage for unforgettable moments shared with friends and loved ones.

And for those special occasions, like weddings, it seamlessly integrates with our billiards room, providing the perfect setting for groomsmen to gather and prepare for the big day in style.

Experience the power of collaboration and connection at the Lower South Class Room—where every gathering is an opportunity to learn, grow, and celebrate together. Reserve your spot today and let the magic of community unfold!
